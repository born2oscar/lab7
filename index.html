What Are Emotional Support Animals?

ESAs provide emotional comfort and companionship without needing special training like service animals, offering critical support to those dealing with mental health issues.

Mental Health Benefits of ESAs

  • Reduction of anxiety and stress
  • Increased production of calming hormones (oxytocin)
  • Improved emotional resilience and coping abilities
  • Reduction of loneliness and isolation

ESAs for College Students

For students juggling academics and social life, ESAs provide stress management, improved focus, emotional well-being, and contribute positively to student life and academic performance.
